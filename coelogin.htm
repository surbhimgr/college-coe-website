About us

CDAC center of excellence (COE) is a team that provides leadership, best practices, research, support and/or training for computer science area.
It is also aimed at revitalizing stalled initiatives. Here at CDAC CoE we collaborate with each other to pursue excellence in technology. Here we
have high performance computer labs which plays an important role in both scientific advancement and economic competitiveness.
